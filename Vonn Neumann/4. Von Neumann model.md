# Introduction-to-programming-UAMI
EN:

The Von Neumann model forms the conceptual basis for the operation of modern computers, establishing the organization and operation of their main components.
Understanding it is fundamental to understanding how programs are executed and how hardware and software interact within a computer system.
:paperclip:

The topic is covered in detail at the following link: [Von Neumann Model](Modelo_Vonn_Neumann.pdf)
